Media Plan Management

All in one place

Brand teams, agencies, publishers and media auditors can easily create, discuss and update media deals within our system. It is applied on a daily basis in ad placement processes and provides access for top managers to track ad placement and media spend.

Request Demo

Use cases

  • Transformation of numerous XLS media plans into a structured, convenient and easily monitored Media Database
  • Real-time reports on Media Campaigns up to ad media
  • Compliance between buying commitments, agreements and media plan execution

Approach

  • The system is designed for various user types: brand, agency, publisher, media auditor each having different access levels
  • Creation of private media databases for individual clients by transforming XLS files into easily manageable database
  • Transformation of media deals into smart contracts, including budget allocation, briefs, media plans, actual media spends, postby reports and correction

Operation process

  • Creation of Media briefs & calendars by brand
  • Team work of brand, agency and publisher on the media plan
  • Campaign execution
  • Approval of postby reports and actual mediaspend by brand
  • Chats between all parties for easier and faster decision making
  • Smooth integration into leading MRM systems (e.g. BrandMaker)

Reports

  • Tracking changes in media database
  • Campaigns execution status
  • Workload per each person: tasks, media plan, campaigns
  • Media calendar
